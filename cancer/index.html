About Us

Leading Cancer Hospital in Thane & Mumbai

Sanjivani Cancer Centre is a premier cancer hospital in Maharashtra, committed to delivering world-class oncological care with compassion. Founded by a team of young, dynamic oncosurgeons, we make cutting-edge cancer treatment accessible and affordable for every patient.

Our centre operates across Mumbai, Thane, Palghar, and Navi Mumbai. With international training from UK and European institutions, our surgeons bring global expertise to local care — offering robotic surgeries, HIPEC, laparoscopic oncology, and personalized treatment for each patient.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the official contact number? +
The contact number for Sanjivani Cancer Centre is +91 8779475032. You can also reach us at contact@sanjivanicancercentre.com.
Who is the founder of Sanjivani Cancer Centre? +
Sanjivani Cancer Centre is owned and founded by Dr. Aditya Manke, an internationally trained Cancer & Laparoscopic Surgeon with credentials from UK and Europe (FRCSEd, MCh, FEBS).
Where is Sanjivani Cancer Centre located? +
We have two main locations: 501-502, Platinum Square, Ghantali Devi Road, Naupada, Thane West 400602 and Mahavir Industrial Estate, Veer Savarkar Marg, Virar East 401305. We also serve Palghar and Navi Mumbai.
Are treatments affordable at Sanjivani? +
Absolutely. Sanjivani was founded with the mission of making advanced oncological surgeries accessible and affordable for all patients, without compromising on quality or cutting-edge techniques.
How does Sanjivani support patients and families? +
Our team believes in a holistic approach — combining medical expertise with emotional and practical support. We create personalized treatment plans and guide patients and families through every step of the cancer journey.
What types of cancer does Sanjivani treat? +
We treat a wide range, including: Breast Cancer, GI Cancer, Gynecological Cancers, Head & Neck Cancer, Hepatobiliary Cancer, and offer treatments like HIPEC/PIPEC, Advanced Laparoscopy, Endoscopy, and more.
